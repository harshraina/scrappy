Behold Scrappy

A place to invest your waste for a social change

Get Started

The smart way to get rid of your scraps

Scrappy is a doorstep service that thrives to help people sell their household junk like old newspapers, iron, pastic books, copies, metals etc. and get paid for it. It is the best platform for the citizens to take part in managing waste and process waste responsibly.

All you have to do is provide the necessary details and we would assign a Scrappy agent to collect the waste at your doorstep

Services and perks

HASSLE-FREE

Users can schedule pickup according to their availability with just a few easy steps.

High Integrity

Accurate weights- our scales are calibrated weekly

Transparent & quick

Pre-decided time for collection; electric weighing machines for accurate weighing; fixed rates leave no scope for malpractice

ENVIRONMENTAL SUSTAINABILITY

Our operations reflect how we integrate environmental sustainability into the business

CASHLESS TRANSACTION

Registered users can accumulate the monthly deals and coupons to encash later; registering with NGOs for scrap donation is also possible.

DOORSTEP PICKUP

An authorised Scrappy agent will come to your doorstep to collect waste ensuring safety and sustainability.
